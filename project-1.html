Project Overview

This is a dynamic replica of MYNTRA, developed during Masai School's Construct Week as part of Unit-4. The project served as an immersive exploration of website fundamentals, utilizing HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Working collaboratively with the Masai Team, the experience was both enjoyable and enlightening, marking our initiation into full-stack web development.

As for the website's functionalities, it boasts full responsiveness, seamless customer signup and login features, advanced product filtering and sorting on the product page, efficient Add to Cart and Wishlist functionalities on item details pages, a clear display of wishlist and cart items, secure storage of customer addresses, and a smooth payment process.
